    Seamless Ticketing with Power Automate: Integrating Outlook and SharePoint for Effortless Workflow Management

    Before implementing this process, there was no formal way to handle PowerOn Specfile requests, which often led to confusion and inefficiency. To address this, I designed a streamlined solution using Microsoft Forms, Power Automate, SharePoint, and Outlook.

    Now, users can fill out a simple form to describe what they’d like to accomplish with their PowerOn script. Once submitted, Power Automate automatically processes the request by capturing all relevant details, adding them to a centralized SharePoint list for tracking, and sending an email notification to the development team.

    Impacts

    This seamless workflow ensures all requests are organized, visible, and actionable, significantly improving communication and efficiency for everyone involved.

    • Improved Organization: Centralized all PowerOn Specfile requests in a SharePoint list, making it easy to track, manage, and prioritize tasks.

    • Enhanced Efficiency: Automated the submission and notification process, reducing manual effort and ensuring faster turnaround times.

    • Streamlined Communication: Ensured developers receive all necessary information via email immediately, minimizing back-and-forth clarification.

    • User-Friendly Accessibility: Provided an intuitive and straightforward form for users, making it easier for them to submit requests without confusion.
